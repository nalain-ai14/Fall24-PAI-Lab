# ⚖️ Wakeel AI - Pakistan's Free AI Legal Aid Assistant

**An intelligent AI-powered legal assistant that helps ordinary Pakistani citizens understand their legal rights in simple language.**

---

## Features

- **Bilingual Support**: Works with English & Roman Urdu
- **Smart Legal Analysis**: Identifies category, relevant Pakistani laws, and sections
- **Case Strength Meter**: Visual 0–100% strength indicator with reasoning
- **Clear Rights & Next Steps**: Easy-to-understand guidance
- **One-Click Document Drafting**: Generates professional Legal Notices
- **Quick Topics**: Instant access to common legal issues
- **Modern UI**: Premium Golden & Black theme
- **Conversation Memory**: Remembers chat history during session

---

## Tech Stack

| Layer       | Technology                  |
|-------------|-----------------------------|
| Backend     | Flask (Python)              |
| AI Engine   | Groq + Llama-3.3-70B        |
| Frontend    | Tailwind CSS + Vanilla JS   |
| Styling     | Golden & Black Premium Theme|
| Deployment  | Ready for Render / Railway  |
